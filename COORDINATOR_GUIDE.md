**Day 1:** Send SMS with link

- Reminder catches procrastinators

For schools with limited internet:
1. Bring tablet with 4G/5G
2. Show QR code on tablet screen
3. Teachers scan with their phones
4. Or use tablet to enroll them directly

**Efficiency:** Enroll 10-15 teachers in 30 minutes

## ‚ùì Common Questions & Answers

### **Q: Teachers don't have smartphones. What do I do?**

**A:** Use one of these methods:
1. **Coordinator-assisted enrollment:** Use your phone/tablet, have teacher dictate their ID and phone
2. **Computer at school:** Open link on school computer
3. **Paper form + manual entry:** Last resort - collect info, enter later

### **Q: How do I know if my link/QR code is working?**

**A:** Test it yourself:
1. Scan the QR code with your phone
2. Check that training details appear correctly
3. Don't submit (unless testing enrollment)

### **Q: Can teachers enroll in multiple trainings at once?**

**A:** Yes! They can:
1. Use QR/link for first training (fastest)
2. Use browse page to add more trainings
3. Or you can share multiple QR codes

**Note:** System will detect schedule conflicts and warn them

### **Q: What if training fills up?**

**A:**
1. System will stop accepting enrollments at max capacity
2. Consider creating a waitlist (contact admin)
3. Or increase capacity if venue allows

### **Q: How do I send reminders?**

**A:**
1. Export non-enrolled teachers list (from admin)
2. Send targeted SMS/email to that list
3. Include deadline urgency: "Only 3 days left!"

## üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

### **Problem: QR code doesn't scan**

**Solutions:**
1. Make sure QR is large enough (min 2cm x 2cm printed)
2. Ensure good lighting
3. Hold phone steady, 10-20cm away
4. Try different QR scanner app
5. Use direct link as backup

### **Problem: Teachers can't click link in SMS**

**Solutions:**
1. SMS apps vary - some don't make links clickable
2. Tell teachers to: Copy link ‚Üí Paste in browser
3. Or: Send via WhatsApp where links are always clickable

### **Problem: Teachers entered wrong info**

**Solutions:**
1. They can re-enroll with correct info (system will update)
2. Or contact admin to manually update
3. Train teachers to double-check before submitting

### **Problem: Teachers don't know their Teacher ID**

**Solutions:**
1. Provide ID lookup via phone/SMS
2. Keep a roster with IDs at meetings
3. Teachers can ask their school admin
